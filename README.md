BRIEF NOTE OF SRI GOWTHAMI JEEVAKARUNYA SANGHAM, RAJAMAHENDRAVARAM.
ORIGIN & OBJECTS:
	In the year 1940 a seva sangham called as Sri Gowthami Jeevakarunya Sangham, Rajamahendravaram was Established on the Bund of River Godavari by the local elders like Dr.A.B.Nageswara Rao and Dr.D.S.Raju Headed by Sri Swamy Karunyananda Swamiji with the following noble objects,
1)Orphanage for boys
2)Home for Orphanage destitutes and handicapped
3)Leprosy Hospital
4)Home for Leprosy patients
5)Making arrangements for cremation of uncared dead bodies
6)Service to the flood cyclones and Fire victims
7)Spreading the message of equality of religious
8)Awareness programmes
9)Women Welfare Programmes
10)Home for old age people
